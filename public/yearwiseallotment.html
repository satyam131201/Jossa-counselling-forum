Analysis of Seat allotment per year

From the above graph, seat allotments generally increased from 2016 to 2018, with a significant jump in the seats allotted during this period. From 2018 to 2020, there are fluctuations in seat allotment numbers, with some years showing an increase while others offer a slight decrease. In 2021, there is a decrease in the number of seat allotments compared to the previous year. In 2022, there is a notable increase in seat allotments compared with last year and a higher number than any other year in the data provided. The seats allotted are increasing due to colleges introducing new branches and growing student intake capacity. Also, Adjustments in reservation policies, such as raising new categories or changes in seat quotas, can impact seat allotments. The COVID-19 pandemic, which began in 2020, also affected seat allotments in subsequent years. Changes in the number of applicants, modifications in admission processes, and uncertainties related to the pandemicThese changes affect the distribution of seats across different types and result in variations in seat allotments.
